Projects

Production Improvement · Micron
Automated Routine Quals Triggering — TEL Equipment

Identified and resolved a critical production issue where routine qualification runs on TEL equipment were not being auto-triggered due to a logic change. This caused entire Mainframes and chambers to sit idle without detection silently impacting throughput and tool utilisation. Led a cross-functional effort to diagnose the root cause through logic review and discussion, implement corrective changes, and establish monitoring protocols to prevent recurrence.

Cross-functional teams involved:
Area Team PO EO Production NPI SIA CH Team
◆  Reduced occurrence rate by 90%  —  from ~50% recurrence down to near-zero after logic fix and sustained monitoring
Process Automation · Micron
ATMS — Auto Targeting Maintenance System

Contributed to the deployment of ATMS, an automated targeting maintenance system designed to accelerate chamber recovery after Preventive Maintenance (PM) and Corrective Maintenance (CM). The system automates inline and RDA inspection checks, enabling tools to be released directly back to production without dependency on the Process team significantly compressing recovery time and reducing bottlenecks on the production floor.

Key impact areas:
PM / CM Recovery Inline Inspection RDA Automation Tool Release Cross-team Efficiency
◆  Achieved 80% efficiency in deployment year — with ongoing improvements driven by continuous team feedback
Digital Transformation · Micron
Smart Passdown System — Automated Production Records

Led the initiative to digitalise and automate the shift passdown process, replacing manual Excel-based records with a system that automatically logs all targeting requirements for production releases. The solution eliminates data entry errors, accelerates feedback loops, and removes unnecessary involvement from the Process team by auto-validating tool release conditions including level, part type, and attribute checks. Designed to safeguard information integrity across every shift handover.

Key impact areas:
Shift Passdown Auto-validation Production Releases Error Prevention Process Efficiency
◆  Prevents missing information  ·  Reduces Process team dependency  ·  Saves time & cost  ·  Increases output quality
